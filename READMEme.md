Privacy Policy for Jamal Network
Last Updated: March 27, 2026

At Jamal Network, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information we may collect. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within our Kuwaiti platform specialized in Purebred Arabian Horses.

1. Information We Collect
Personal Information: Currently, the app is a display platform. We do not require users to create an account or provide personal details like names or phone numbers for general browsing.

Admin Data: Only authorized administrators using the hidden login panel provide credentials (username/password) to manage content.

Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information such as device type and OS version to improve the app's performance.

2. Data Usage
The information collected is used solely to:

Provide and maintain the service.

Update horse show schedules and gallery content.

Monitor app stability and fix technical issues.

3. Data Tracking (NSUserTrackingUsageDescription)
While our app includes technical frameworks for tracking, we strictly use this for:

Improving user experience.

Understanding which sections of the app (e.g., horse galleries or show timings) are most visited.
We do not sell your data to third-party advertisers.

4. Third-Party Services
We use Google Firebase to host our data and images. Firebase may collect certain standard diagnostic information as part of its cloud services.

5. Your Rights
As a user, you have the right to use the app without providing personal data. Since we do not store personal profiles for general users, there is no personal data to delete or modify.
